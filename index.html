Specializing in travel and adventure photography

Gary Rapaich is a Colorado based photographer who focuses on landscape and portrait photography. Gary is an avid alpine adventurer who is available for hire to capture your hiking, rock climbing, and backcountry travel adventures. He also has avalanche safety training, wilderness first aid training, and is an avid mountaineer.His past photography trips include photographing Iceland in the winter, hiking to the Mount Everest Base camp, and a climbing trip to Mount Blanc in France. Gary is also available for hire to take portrait photography including couples and graduation pictures. To have a creative experience to capture your special trip or travel experience, contact Gary Rapaich so he can describe your journey in photographic detail. Gary edits images to assure they are of the highest quality and takes into consideration outside lighting and color. He has considerable knowledge of Photoshop and photo retouching to provide you with an exemplary experience. Gary prioritizes communication with his clients to avoid any errors and combines his professional career with his freelance ideals. Collaborating with clients is a priority in the selection and retouching of images. In addition Gary welcomes dogs and other pets to increase customer satisfaction.
